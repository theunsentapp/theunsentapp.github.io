Privacy Policy
Effective Date: May 26, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 26, 2026

1. Who We Are

unsent. is a mobile application developed and operated by The Unsent App, based in San Francisco, California. We provide a private, safe space for users to express their thoughts, feelings, and unspoken words.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: theunsentapp@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address and password when you create an account
  • Date of birth (used to verify age and apply appropriate protections)
  • Letters, voice notes, and other content you create within the app
  • Posts you choose to share to the community forum
  • Reports you submit about other users
  • Any information you provide when contacting support

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data and crash reports
  • IP address
  • General location (city/region level only, not precise location)

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Your full name (unless you choose to provide it)
  • Precise GPS location
  • Contacts or address book
  • Photos outside of what you explicitly upload

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Deliver the core features of the app
  • Keep the platform safe by reviewing reports of harmful behavior
  • Send important account and safety notifications
  • Improve the app and fix bugs
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT use your information to sell to third parties, serve targeted advertising, build advertising profiles, or share with data brokers.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest. Your private letters and voice notes are never shared without your explicit consent.

5. Who We Share Your Information With

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who help us operate the app (Supabase, Anthropic) bound by strict data processing agreements
  • Law enforcement if required by law or to protect the safety of our users
  • In a business transfer, with advance notice to you

We never sell your personal information.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Users under the age of 13 are not permitted to use this app. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Users between 13 and 17 have access to a modified experience with additional safety protections.

If you believe your child under 13 has created an account, contact us immediately at theunsentapp@gmail.com and we will delete the account and all associated data.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your data, opt out of the sale of your information (we do not sell personal information), and be free from discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at theunsentapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 45 days.

8. Your Choices and Controls

  • Delete your account and all associated data from within app settings
  • Delete individual letters, voice notes, or posts
  • Change your email address or password
  • Request a copy of your data by emailing us

9. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law. Community posts that are part of an active safety investigation may be retained for up to 90 days after account deletion.

10. Third Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic Claude API — powers the Luna AI companion
  • Expo / EAS — app delivery infrastructure

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date and notify you via email or in-app notification if the changes are significant.
